About Me

Hi my name is Tim Rolston and this is my web portfolio. I am a computer science student currently studying at the University of Victoria who loves to explore the creative side of developing and coding. In my free time I am usually camping and exploring outside while enjoying a good cup of coffee. Here you will find some of the previous professional work I've done as well as some of my personal projects that I work on.
